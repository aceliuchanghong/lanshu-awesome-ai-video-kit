# 10 · 五大主流模型对比与选型

> 不同的视频生成模型有各自的"性格"，提示词风格差异巨大。**先选对模型，再写提示词** — 比"硬把一种写法套到所有模型"靠谱 10 倍。

## 选型决策树

```
我想要什么？
├── 中文剧情、原生音频 + 多角色对话
│   ├── 中文背景 + 物理感强        → Kling 3.0
│   └── 多人对话 + 精确音效同步     → Veo 3.1 ★
│
├── 极致电影质感（35mm/胶片/复杂分层）
│   ├── 一次成片 + 对白 + 高级摄影  → Sora 2 ★
│   └── 多镜头切换 + 复杂叙事       → Seedance 2.0
│
├── 5-8 秒紧凑短片
│   └── 要原生环境音 + 严格执行     → HappyHorse 1.0
│
├── 图生视频（已有参考图）
│   ├── 强物理感 + 自由控制         → Kling 3.0 (Motion Brush)
│   └── 一般用途                    → Seedance / Veo 也可
│
└── 病毒短视频 / 变身 / 抽象艺术
    └── 中文创意 + Tiktok 风        → Kling 3.0
```

## 公式对照（同一需求不同模型）

需求："一个穿红裙的女子在屋顶跳舞"

### Seedance 2.0（8 要素）
> A young woman in a flowing red silk dress dances barefoot on a rooftop at golden hour. Slow 360-degree orbit shot, low angle. Warm tungsten bounce light from city lights below. Hair and dress catch wind motion. Photorealistic, cinematic quality, 35mm film grain. No watermark, no logo, face stability preserved.

### HappyHorse 1.0（30-55 词 + audio）
> A young woman in a flowing red silk dress dancing on a city rooftop at golden hour, slow circular tracking shot, warm side light, hair and dress flowing, with soft wind and distant traffic audible. Cinematic, naturalistic, 8 seconds.

### Kling 3.0（5 层进阶）
> Scene: A brick tenement rooftop at golden hour, fairy bulbs strung overhead.
> Characters: A young woman in flowing red silk dress, barefoot, curls catching sunlight.
> Action: She spins; the dress flares; she pauses, smiling, looks toward the city.
> Camera: Slow dolly-in then 270-degree orbit, low angle.
> Audio & Style: Soft accordion music + distant city hum. 1970s vintage romantic drama.

### Sora 2（Shot List 分层）
> Style: 1970s romantic drama, 35mm film with warm halation and gate weave.
> Inside: rooftop stage at golden hour, fairy bulbs hum overhead.
> Cinematography: Medium-wide, slow dolly-in then 270° orbit. 40mm spherical lens, shallow focus.
> Actions:
> - She spins; dress flares catching sunlight
> - She pauses, smiling, glances at the city
> - Sheets drift across frame, briefly veiling skyline
> Background Sound: Wind, fabric flutter, distant traffic, faint accordion.

### Veo 3.1（8 元素 + Audio）
> A young woman in a flowing red silk dress dances barefoot on a brick rooftop at golden hour. Slow circular dolly shot, low angle, 40mm cinematic feel. Warm side-lighting from sunset and fairy bulbs above. She spins, the dress flares, then she pauses and smiles at the city skyline. Dialogue: she whispers "The city always listens." Audio: Soft accordion music, distant car horns, fabric flutter in the wind, her gentle breathing.

## 跨模型通用守则

1. **主体先行**：人物的稳定特征（服装、发型）写在前面，**永远不依赖代词**。
2. **动词具体化**：用 `rockets / slams / spins / dips` 替代 `moves / goes`。
3. **指定光源**：用 `neon sign / candlelight / fluorescent tubes`，不要 `dramatic lighting`。
4. **加 endpoint**：动作要有终止状态（`then settles`），防止模型陷入无限循环。
5. **音频显式化**：能写 audio 就写（Veo/Kling/HappyHorse 原生支持；Sora 也支持；Seedance 需后期）。
6. **风格锚定**：用具体的视觉锚点（电影、艺术家、时代）取代 "cinematic"。

## 何时切换模型

| 当前问题 | 建议切换到 |
|---|---|
| Seedance 出来人脸抖 / 切镜不稳 | Sora 2 / Kling 3.0 |
| HappyHorse 提示词写不下了 | Kling 3.0（中长视频） |
| Kling 中文场景里英文台词 + 多人对话 | Veo 3.1 |
| Sora 2 不能用（地区/账号限制） | Kling 3.0 / Veo 3.1 |
| Veo 3 视频太短不够剧情 | Veo 3.1（最长 60s）/ Kling 智能分镜 |
